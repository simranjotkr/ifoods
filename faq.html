PURCHASE, ORDERING, & RETURNS

+

iFood is happy to redeem all coupons, price-reduction offers, online purchase bonuses, and special discounts.

When placing your online order please follow instructions for coupon and bonus redemption.

You can also contact Customer Service at 1-800-iFOOD between 9 AM and 5 PM, EST, Monday through Friday to redeem offers.

+

Our standard shipping time is 1 to 3 hours from the receipt of your purchase order. If your order requires expedited delivery, please call Customer Service at 1-800-iFOOD.

+

Our products are satisfaction guaranteed. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, please contact Customer Service within 7 days of delivery and we will gladly replace your products or credit your account.
